About Stickywrite

We’re a forward-thinking team building a writing app that simplifies the process through user-first design. Stickywrite’s node-based approach lets you build ideas piece by piece in a more modular way.

Stickywrite was created to solve a problem traditional writing tools don’t: not everyone wants to write inside one giant document from start to finish. If you prefer working in chunks/scenes, beats, notes, character ideas. Stickywrite is built for that.

What makes it different

  • Modular writing: works like sticky notes, but easy to reorder and connect.
  • Bookmarks: quickly jump to chapters, TODO spots, or gaps to fill.
  • Works offline: ideal for commutes (yes, even on the London Underground).
  • Free: with a small banner ad; remove it with a pocket change, one-time fee.
  • Your files are yours: saved locally and easy to export, share, and keep private.

Why it “clicks” for some writers

A lot of apps are built to keep you scrolling. Stickywrite flips that: the same swipe-and-move feeling can be used to move through your own story,notes, scenes, character sheets, and worldbuilding,without the doom-scroll. Color-coding helps you instantly separate “Needs attention”, “Character”, “World”, or “Gap” notes.

From phone to desktop

Don’t want to write the final manuscript on your phone? No problem. Export your work to a .txt file and drop it straight into your favorite word processor when you’re ready.
